Chicken Tikka Shawarma

Description

This recipe is a combination of the ever-popular shawarma hailing from the Middle East and the delicious Tikka Masala from India. From my personal experience, shawarmas are typically a bit dry, spiceless, and sometimes lacking flavor, while Chicken Tikka Masala seems to deliver so much flavor that you can get tired of it after a bit. The rich spices and creaminess of the masala will work to balance out the dryness of the shawarma, and vice versa. It's a match made in heaven!

Ingredients

For Chicken

Marinade

  • 28 oz Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast, chopped up into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp ground Cumin
  • 1.5 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (or a different chili powder)
  • 1 tsp Paprika (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1.25 cups heavy or thickened cream
  • 14 oz tomato puree (or tomatoes to blend in the cream)
  • 1.5 tbsp garlic, finely grated
  • Same amounts of the spices listed in the "Marinade" Section
  • Cilantro/coriander for garnish

For Shawarma

  • Pita wrap
  • Tahini Sauce or:
  • 0.5 cup Tahini paste + 1 tbsp olive oil + 0.5 lemon, juiced + salt
  • Olives, smashed
  • Peppers
